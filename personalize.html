Frequently Asked Questions

What is Personify? +
Personify is a platform that helps you manage and organize your job applications effectively without the need for long spreadsheets and constantly checking your emails.
How do I get started? +
Once you register as a Personify user, you should be able to see your list of applications scanned from your email account. Click on the search engine to filter out your applications for easier navigation, and explore the other pages in our account! Hope you enjoy our product!
What tech stack did we use for this project? (Inspiration moment) +
The tech stack we used for this project includes:
  • Frontend: Figma, HTML, CSS (TailwindCSS), JavaScript (jQuery)
  • Backend RESTful API: Flask
  • Email Scanning: Gmail API
  • Deriving Application Statuses: NLP
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0, JWT
Is my data secure? +
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Some of the measures include:
  • Secure MFA
  • Encrypted storage (Client-Side = SHA-256, Server-Side = MongoDB Encryption mechanisms)
  • Regular security audits to ensure your information remains private and safe
